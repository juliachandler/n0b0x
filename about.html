Why N0B0X?

My motivation for N0B0X is to provide support and guidance to those interested in improving personal relationships, exploring alternative modes of lifestyle, or experiencing critical life transition or adversity. I am a statistical programmer, naturalist, professor, creator, stock market enthusiast and cancer survivor with extensive experience in trauma recovery and CPTSD management.

To me N0B0X means no boundaries, infinite expansion. What could it mean to you?

Mission Statement

With a compassionate inquiry approach, we may consciously deconstruct the mental, emotional, spiritual and physical schema built on past experience. We can then reveal new paths and are freed into futures that are currently gated by our own present state. It is the mission of Dr. Chandler and selected N0B0X participants to open the choice of this path to those interested in personal empowerment, relational growth and alternative living.
